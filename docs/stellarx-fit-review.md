# StellarX Fit Review

The civic platform is useful on its own, but it becomes a stronger StellarX project when Stellar is part of the core trust layer.

## Best Fit

The strongest direction is proof-of-payment for government services.

Each government service fee can create a Stellar payment transaction. The transaction hash becomes a permanent receipt that citizens and staff can verify.

## Why This Fits

The current architecture already includes:

- Multitenant organization structure
- Citizen accounts
- Services directory
- Request tracking
- Staff dashboard
- Public portal

The Stellar extension can add:

- Service fee management
- Tenant Stellar Testnet wallet settings
- Payment intent records
- SEP-7 payment QR generation
- Transaction hash verification
- Public receipt pages
- Staff payment dashboard

## Avoid Weak Positioning

Do not position the app as a generic payment app, donation app, crowdfunding tool, voting app, DAO, escrow service, or NFT project.

The stronger positioning is:

> A civic trust platform for verifiable government service payments, public receipts, and transparent service records.
