# Product Review

## Product Positioning

CivicTrust is a civic service operating system for LGUs, barangays, municipalities, and provincial teams. It combines a public citizen portal with a structured staff backend.

## Why It Makes Sense

Citizens need a simple way to submit concerns and track progress. Staff need a clear workflow for routing requests, updating statuses, and managing public information.

The platform solves both sides:

- Citizens get visibility and reference numbers.
- Staff get a report queue and content management.
- Organizations get a scalable tenant model.

## Current Strengths

- Clear public portal
- Report tracking
- Citizen accounts
- Staff dashboard
- Tenant-scoped data
- Public service content
- Emergency contacts
- PostgreSQL deployment readiness

## Most Important Next Improvement

Before adding many features, the strongest next product milestone is a Stellar proof-of-payment module for government service fees.

This gives the product a more differentiated story:

> Civic services with verifiable payment receipts and transparent records.
