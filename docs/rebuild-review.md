# Rebuild Review

The project is structured as a full civic services platform rather than a static landing page.

## Covered Areas

- Public city portal
- Citizen reports
- Request tracking
- Services directory
- News and announcements
- Hotlines
- Citizen accounts
- Staff operations
- Content management
- Tenant settings
- PostgreSQL deployment

## Product Direction

The application is positioned as a civic SaaS platform that can evolve into a Stellar-powered civic trust product through verifiable government service payments and public receipt records.
