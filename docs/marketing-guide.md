# Marketing Guide

## Positioning

CivicTrust helps public sector teams modernize citizen services through one clear portal for reports, request tracking, public information, and operational workflows.

## Main Promise

> Citizens get visibility. Staff get structure. Public service teams get a trusted digital operating layer.

## Target Customers

- LGUs
- Barangays
- Municipalities
- Provincial offices
- Civic service contractors
- Public works and public safety teams

## Differentiators

- Citizen-facing portal plus staff backend
- Multitenant architecture
- Clear request tracking
- Public update timeline
- Built-in public CMS
- Ready path toward Stellar-backed payment receipts

## Sales Angle

Start with report management and citizen communication. Expand into paid services, verifiable receipts, and transparent public records.

## Suggested Pricing Direction

- Starter: small barangay or office
- Professional: full city/municipality service portal
- Enterprise: multi-office or provincial deployment
- Add-on: Stellar payment receipt module
